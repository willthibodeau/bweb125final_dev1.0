Urban Trail Asheville

The Asheville Urban Trail is a history and art museum without walls. It is a walk through time with an opportunity to examine buildings, study neighborhoods and learn about the people and events gives Asheville its distinctivie habitude. The Trail provides visitors and residents an opportunity to learn intriguing stories about the city's past while explorign a wealth of shops, eateries, and galleries, along with beautiful architecture and lively public spaces. It's a celebration of what makes America a fun place to live.
